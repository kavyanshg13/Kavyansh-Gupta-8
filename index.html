COURSES

I recently completed a web development course provided by a company called MyCAPTAIN. This course was designed to provide comprehensive knowledge and hands-on experience in various aspects of web development. The course began with an introduction to the fundamentals of web development, including HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It covered the basics of creating and styling web pages, and also delved into more advanced topics such as responsive design and user interface development. Throughout the course, we were exposed to various web development tools and technologies, such as Bootstrap, jQuery, and Git. By the end of the course, I had acquired a solid foundation in web development and gained the skills necessary to create functional and visually appealing websites.
